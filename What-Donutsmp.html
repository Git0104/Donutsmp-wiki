What is Donutsmp ​

Q.What is Donutsmp? ​

A.Donutsmp is one of the largest Minecraft survival servers in the world. ​

Donutsmp is one of the world's largest survival multiplayer servers, operated by DrDonut.
Donutsmp is a server where player-versus-player (PvP) combat and base destruction are allowed.

Q.How to join Donutsmp? ​

The server address of Donutsmp is donutsmp.net.
The server address for the Bedrock (integrated) version is bedrock.donutsmp.net,
and the port is 19132.
